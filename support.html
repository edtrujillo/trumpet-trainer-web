Support

Email us at support@contactplus.com and we'll get back to you. When reporting an issue, it helps to include your device model, OS version, and the app version (Settings → About).

Frequently asked

Is there a subscription?

No. Standard and Pro are one-time purchases. Free lets you practice the scale libraries right away.

What's the difference between Standard and Pro?

Standard unlocks every song, lets you import songs and libraries, and share individual songs. Pro adds everything in Standard plus creating, deleting, and exporting whole libraries.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Open the paywall (tap any locked content or Settings → Subscription) and choose Restore purchases. Purchases restore for the same Apple ID / Google account that bought them.

How do I change note names or the app language?

Settings → Note names (Letters / German / Solfège) and Settings → Language. Note naming can be set independently of the app language.
