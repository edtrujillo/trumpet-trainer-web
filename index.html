Built for how you actually practice

🎵 Play-along loops

Eight measures on the staff, played back with a piano-style chord under each measure — train fingerings in a musical context, not in silence.

🎯 Real-time scoring

Get credit for holding the right valves as each note plays, with a clean-run detector that rewards mistake-free passes.

👆 Swipe to control

Swipe left/right to change tempo, up/down to transpose to any key. The staff and chords follow instantly.

📚 Built-in libraries

Major and minor scales, chromatics, arpeggios, technical studies, and more — organized by topic.

📈 Track your progress

Daily, weekly, and monthly trends, streaks, per-song stats, and unlockable badges keep you coming back.

🎼 Read music your way

Note names in letters (C D E), German (H / B), or fixed-do solfège (Do Re Mi) — switch independently of the app language.

Available in English, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Italian, and Japanese.
